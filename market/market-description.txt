Have your Machinecoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Machinecoin Wallet is the first mobile Machinecoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Machinecoin amount in MAC and mMAC.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Machinecoin via NFC, QR-codes or Machinecoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Machinecoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Machinecoin balance.

You can find more about Machinecoin at
http://machinecoin.org

If you want to contribute to Machinecoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/machinecoin-project/machinecoin-android-wallet
